PHASE 8D

Dataset Quality Inspector

====================================================

GOAL

Create an interactive quality inspection workspace allowing researchers to manually inspect extracted images before annotation.

This is a standalone module inside the Dataset Engineering Toolkit.

It is NOT part of FastAPI.

It is NOT part of React.

It is NOT part of YOLO inference.
