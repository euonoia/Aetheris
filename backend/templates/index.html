Load a road/traffic video, or start your webcam, to begin tracking. Frames are streamed to the FastAPI server for detection.
You can draw multiple zones of every kind — each click-pair adds a new one instead of replacing the last. Stop line: 2 clicks per line. Allowed direction: 2 clicks (arrow). No-parking zone: 2 clicks (rectangle corners). Speed gate pair: click 2 points for Gate A, then 2 points for Gate B, then enter the real-world distance between them. Every tracked vehicle also gets a continuous rough speed estimate from pixel movement (using the scale field) even with no gates drawn. Motorcycles are additionally inspected for rider count and helmet use by the server's AI review, if configured.
